Q: How do I launch the Pencil Launcher?

A: How to list:
Method #1 (best) - Launch Lead Reservoir Tube:

  1. Place pad of thumb firmly on eraser.
  2. Press down as far as you want.
  3. Quickly move finger straight left or right (whichever is more comfortable) off of the eraser.
Method #2 (not as good but okay) - Launch Pencil Body:
  1. Place pad of thumb on top of eraser with your non-dominant hand with pencil tip pointing upwards.
  2. Grab upper pencil body (near clip) with dominant hand using two fingers.
  3. Push pencil body towards eraser direction.
  4. When pencil body is as far down as possible, slide fingers down body towards eraser until the body tube releases.

PROCESS

SINGLE SPRING

  1. Tip of pencil is removed.
  2. Lead Reservoir Tube is pushed down and the Chuck is forced upwards towards the Chuck Ring, allowing lead to be shaken out.
  3. Button is pushed to low position and the Chuck is broken off.
  4. Outer pencil housing is used to bend part of the Chuck Ring and Lead Reservoir Tube out of place.
  5. Push Tip of Lead Reservoir Tube is slid out.
  6. One Use Pencil launcher created.
  7. Special process carried out, allowing spring to get locked in place.
  8. Multi-use Modded Pencil Launcher created.

DUAL SPRING+

  1. Start from Single Spring Multi-use Modded Pencil.
  2. Extract spring from another mechanical pencil.
  3. Special process allows springs to bond, creating one bigger spring.
  4. Multi-use Dual Spring Pencil Launcher created.
  5. Process repeated for more springs if needed.
